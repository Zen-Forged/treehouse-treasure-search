What's happening here

The tabs changed meaning. Not "available vs sold" any longer — two ways of looking at the same available inventory. Window View is the first-glance gestalt (glance through the booth's window, see nine things). Shelf View is the slower walk (scroll the row, linger on each one).

Sold items don't render on this page. The "Found homes" tab retires. Sold items still exist in Supabase; they just don't surface here yet. FoundTile stays in ShelfGrid.tsx with a retention comment — a future vendor story surface will want it.

Hero banner retypes but doesn't restructure. The eyebrow becomes IM Fell italic; the vendor name becomes IM Fell 30px. The dark-green "Booth 369" badge and the MapPin · America's Antique Mall line both retire from the banner — the cartographic block below now carries both.

The cartographic block is load-bearing. Pin + mall + address above, tick, X + booth pill below. Replaces <BoothLocationCTA> entirely. The address is the maps link (dotted underline, no "Directions →" word). The booth pill row wraps a Link — where to, we decide in code review; a "manage booth" owner surface is a separate future question.

Toggle is text-only. Two IM Fell italic links centered, hairline under the active one, "·" between. No pill chrome, no icons. Reads as part of the page's voice, not as dashboard chrome.

AddFindTile is the top-left cell in Window View. Dashed hairline border, matte paperCream fill, small "+" and italic "Add a find" label. Never appears in Shelf View.

ExploreBanner retires. Replaced by a diamond divider + quiet IM Fell italic closer line ("The shelf ends here. More booths await you."). No button — BottomNav's Home does the navigation.
