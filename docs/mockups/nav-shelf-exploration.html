How to compare

Ask of each: Does the nav support the page or compete with it? Does the metaphor register without shouting?

A — The Suggestion. Risk: might feel like "just a brown bar." Reward: safe, won't break anything.

B — The Grain. Risk: grain is either invisible or too loud. Reward: quietly reinforces the wood idea without committing.

C — The Full Shelf. Risk: tips into costume. Reward: genuine "wait, did they just do that?" moment if it lands.

D — The Line Alone. Risk: misses the opportunity. Reward: maximum restraint; metaphor lives in the words Home/Find Map/Booths/My Booth.
