Pick a frame

A — Concept-faithful 2×2. Faithfully renders your reference. Trade-off: a "Coming soon" tile is a tile that doesn't do anything (dead pixels until Messenger ships).

B — Channel stack. Sub-labels carry the why-this-channel weight. Each row is its own unit; future Messenger appends cleanly. Trade-off: less brand-y; reads more like a system list.

C — Compact 3 squares. Header retreats; channels are the hero. Visual quietness. Trade-off: loses the wordmark presence; "treehouse-ness" of the share moment is thinner.

Pick A, B, or C — or a hybrid (e.g., "B but with C's slim header"). Once you pick, V1.5 dial-pass + locked decisions, then ship.
