Net visual delta — small but real

  • Input bg unification: 6 surfaces flip from inkWash to postit. Postit (#fbf3df) is opaque + slightly warmer than inkWash (rgba 255,253,248,0.70). Side-by-side delta is subtle on paperCream backgrounds; more visible on darker sheet backgrounds (sheets retain paperCream bg, so the delta there is also subtle).
  • Label register: 4 surfaces gain a label-size adjustment (13 → 15 on page tier, plus login/email drops italic + inkMuted). Reads as more authoritative + more on-brand vs. session-82's typography pass.
  • Page-tier CTA shadow: 4 surfaces gain a barely-perceptible shadow tightening (14px-blur → 12px-blur · 0.25 → 0.22 on post/preview only). First adoption of v1.shadow.ctaGreen.
  • Sheet disabled bg: EditBoothSheet + AddBoothSheet bump from 0.30 → 0.40 — slightly less translucent. Aligns with v1.greenDisabled token.
  • Setup CTA: loses the wide 14×22 padding for the canonical 15. Modest narrowing.
  • admin/login PIN input: drops the +4px left padding (18 → 14). The PIN input visually shrinks slightly; UX impact zero.

If the visual delta from D2 (login/email label change) feels too far

  • Alternative: keep login/email at 13 upright (compact-tier) since the form is single-input + minimal. Forces an extra "label size doesn't follow tier" rule. Not recommended.
  • My read: pick the converge. 15 reads as authoritative without overwhelming a single-input form.
