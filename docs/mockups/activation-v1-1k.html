Activation Flow — v1.1k

The four Mode C task-first surfaces move from v0.2 Georgia + legacy chrome to v1.1j paper vocabulary. Mockup covers: /vendor-request, /login, /admin/login (new), /setup.

Locked in v1.1k (from design-system.md Status)

  • Mode C grammar — back-arrow paper bubble top-left; no masthead wordmark; no diamonds, no post-it, no cartographic glyphs. Editorial voice for moments; precise voice for data.
  • Paper-wash 60px success bubble — rgba(42,26,10,0.04) bg, 0.5px inkHairline edge, glyph in inkPrimary. Retires the v0.2 greenLight/greenBorder chrome.
  • Filled green CTA — commit actions only: Request access, Email me a code, Sign in as Admin, Go to my shelf. End-of-path → IM Fell italic dotted-underline text links.
  • Form input primitive — white translucent bg, 1px inkHairline border (focus 1.5px inkPrimary, error 1.5px redBorder), 14px radius, FONT_SYS 16px.
  • Email echo line — horizontal row with mail glyph + "Sent to [email]", hairline rules above/below. Not a surface card.
  • Admin PIN retires from /login — new dedicated /admin/login route. /login becomes curator-only (no tabs).
  • Native <select> on vendor-request mall field — MallSheet migration deliberately deferred to Sprint 5.

Open questions for review

  • Logo glyph on /login vs /admin/login — curator gets a green leaf (matches the Treehouse brand mark used on the Home masthead v1.1j), admin gets a shield. Alternative: both use the leaf and admin just uses the Shield inside the CTA button. Pick one.
  • /setup success CTA copy — "Go to my shelf" vs "Open my booth" vs "Start filling the shelf". Currently "Go to my shelf" because it matches the BottomNav label the vendor is about to see.
  • /setup success headline — "Welcome to your shelf, David." vs "Your shelf is ready, David." vs "Welcome to Treehouse, David." The current choice grounds the moment in their specific shelf rather than the product name, which felt more personal.
  • Email echo address break behavior — long emails wrap to a second line via word-break: break-all. On the success screen in 1B the test email wraps the "+10" onto a second line. Acceptable, or should we truncate with ellipsis?
  • Paste-from-clipboard treatment — current: italic IM Fell text link with a small green clipboard glyph inline. Alternative: hide the glyph entirely, just the italic link. Pick one.
  • Error bubble color on 4C — currently retoned to red. Alternative: keep it paper-wash (inkPrimary alert glyph on a plain bubble) and let only the alert glyph carry the warning. Pick one.

What's deliberately NOT shown

  • MallSheet on /vendor-request — deferred to Sprint 5 per v1.1k (h). Field uses native <select> in 1A and 5.
  • /admin full page — out of scope per v1.1k (g). Only the surgical edit to its unauth-gate Sign-in button (pointing at /admin/login) lands with this sprint.
  • PWA "add to home screen" prompt — Sprint 5, unchanged.
  • Curator rename (/login → /curators, "Curator Sign in") — the tag-line voice is already in place ("Curator Sign in"). The route rename is Sprint 5.
