3C — Render as a normal find (no mark)
SERVES Zero extra code. Zero marketing-chrome risk. Find Detail looks normal whether sold or not. The vendor would have to mark it sold AND it would have to not get filtered at the feed/map level.
COSTS Dishonest. A shopper could drive to the booth for a sold piece. The price shown is fiction. Breaks trust.
NOTES Not recommended — shown for completeness. Violates "rooted in reality."

Remaining pick

Pick one Find Detail sold-state variant: 3A, 3B, or 3C. My recommendation: 3B — it's the cleanest match to the journal voice ("Visit the shelf →" style closer) and doesn't show a price that isn't real. 3A is a reasonable compromise if you want the photograph to be visible one last time; the auto-redirect question ("redirect after Ns?" vs "stay until tap") is worth deciding if we go 3A.
