Login triage v1

Two-screen split: /login = triage (returning vendor vs new vendor), /login/email = the existing OTP form, refined. Two structural choices to pick from.

Decisions already locked

  • Routing: /login = triage (Screen 1). /login/email = sign-in form (Screen 2). BottomNav Profile-tap → /login (mental model preserved).
  • "Create Vendor Account" routes to /vendor-request — canonical onboarding surface (titled "Create your digital booth" since session 91).
  • No Google OAuth in V1. Removed from screen 2 entirely. Separate decision later — needs Supabase provider config + account-linking strategy.
  • Magic-link callback stays at /login (path a). Triage page detects ?confirmed=1 and renders the existing confirming bridge instead of triage cards. No changes to lib/auth.ts or email templates needed.
  • Vendor-only framing for V1. After R1 (shopper accounts) ships, Screen 1 will gain a third "I'm shopping" card. Screen 2 title "Vendor Sign in" will need to generalize then.
  • Orphan-auth-user behavior preserved. If a brand-new email enters the OTP form and verifies, Supabase still creates an auth.users row. Triage solves discovery — orphan-detection redirect is a follow-on.
  • Card tap target: entire card is tappable + button is the visual CTA. iOS-native pattern (forgiving large hit area + clear primary action).
  • Wordmark asset: existing public/wordmark.png (the tree-and-text logo). No illustrated tree, no footer illustration.
  • Sign-out stays on Screen 2 as italic dotted-link below the form (current location). Authed users hitting /login directly are handled per Q2.
  • Pending-vendor-request state (submitted but unapproved) not specially surfaced. They sign in normally; /my-shelf empty state handles the no-booth case.

What this mockup is asking you to judge

  • Screen 1 hero treatment — Frame 1 (wordmark + "Welcome to Treehouse Finds" hero text) vs. Frame 2 (wordmark larger, no hero text — wordmark IS the title).
  • Screen 2 form refinements — bottom "Create a vendor account" callout card replaces the old "First time? An account is created automatically." line. Sign-in CTA copy: "Email me a sign-in code" vs. current "Email me a code". Title: "Vendor Sign in" stays as-is.
  • Authed-user state on /login — Frame 4 alternative (triage with "Manage my booth" / "Sign out" cards) vs. current behavior (skip triage, go straight to Screen 2 with sign-out below the form).
