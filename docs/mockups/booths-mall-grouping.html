Booths — Mall Grouping Options

Both options keep the 2-column grid. The question is how the mall separator reads between groups.

What are we deciding?

A — Section headers: Each mall gets an IM Fell title with a hairline rule extending to the right and a faint booth count on the far end. The rule visually bridges across both columns. Clean, editorial, matches the journal voice of the rest of the app.

B — Location pills: Each mall gets a small frosted pill (📍 + name + count) sitting above its grid group. Lower visual weight than the rule — the pill is contained rather than spanning the full width. Feels more like a tag or filter chip than a section title.
