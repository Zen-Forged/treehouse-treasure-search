Each saved find as a self-contained "I encountered this at a real place" entry. Vertical stack, full-width Lora title, polaroid retains current shape, "Explore the booth →" CTA wires to /shelf/[slug].

Two structural variants — same underlying find row.
  • Frame A — Per-find provenance (no grouping). Every saved find row carries its own cartographic block + "Explore the booth →" CTA, even when adjacent saves share a booth. Strongest expression of "destination per find."
  • Frame B — Grouped by booth. Cartographic + CTA hoisted to a section header above same-booth saves. Find rows underneath are lighter (polaroid + title + price only). Cleaner rhythm; relies on the header to anchor place.
Sample data: 5 saves across 2 booths at 1 mall (Bluegrass Antique Mall) — Cardinal Curiosities (3 saves) and Old Soul Provisions (2 saves).
