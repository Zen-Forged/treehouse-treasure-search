What's held constant across A/B/C

  • User-pin treatment — filled green leaf inside cream halo with a soft pulsing ring (branded leaf vocabulary per Q3=B). Variants surface as prose Q after frame pick: pulsing vs static halo; leaf-in-circle vs leaf-on-stake; saturated green vs mid-green.
  • Arrow placement on PinCallout — 30×30 flanking chevron bubbles outside the callout (clear spatial-navigation affordance, not entity-action). Alternate: integrated inside the callout footer next to Explore/Navigate. Surfaces as prose Q after frame pick.
  • Carousel position — fixed-positioned floating above map area, bottom: 100px (above BottomNav's 22px offset + 56px height + 22px gap). Drawer geometry unchanged; carousel sits on top of the map area visually at z-index between drawer (30) and BottomNav (50).
  • Carousel ↔ pin sync — tap card → pin selects + map flies; tap pin → carousel scrolls to that card; left/right arrows on callout → step through carousel order (nearest→furthest).
  • Selected-state visual — Frame A: green outline + 3px lift. Frame B: green border + 3px lift. Frame C: green ring around photo + 2px lift. All three show the selected card as the leftmost / first in the scroll order.
